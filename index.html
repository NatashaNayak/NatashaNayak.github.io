Pricing Strategy for Recurring Revenue

A deeper example of how I combine pricing logic, analytical modeling, strategic thinking, and executive communication.

Context: Thermo Fisher was evaluating pricing and subscription opportunities to improve customer lifetime value and recurring revenue potential.

My Role: Contributed to market research, pricing analysis, customer segmentation, business case development, and executive-ready recommendations.

Approach: Diagnosed customer value drivers, evaluated subscription pricing structures, analyzed CLV implications, and translated findings into strategic recommendations.

Outcome: Recommendations projected a 35% increase in CLV and approximately $14M in incremental recurring revenue opportunity.
